About Us

The Northern Luzon ICT Research Congress is an event conducted every year in Saint Louis University. It enables top SLU students and people from other schools to present innovative projects that could be beneficial to the community. This years Northern Luzon ICT Research Congress will be the 12th.

What is Our Goal?

  • Introduce research development
  • Share researches with the community
  • Serve as inspiration for 3rd years in their thesis
  • Open collaboration with other schools
